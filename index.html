Careers

At Flower by Sarah we look to hire extraordinary people with a “can do” attitude. We recruit, train and develop the best people. We look for people who go out of their way to make their guests experience a memorable one! We don’t expect you to be “extraordinary” when we hire you but we will invest in you and your training to ensure you are the best. We believe in empowering our staff by giving them the tools, knowledge, training, support and encouragement to give excellent service to our guests. We love to see our staff grow and develop with us and enjoy their jobs as much as we love seeing our guests have a great time with us!
